Our Mission

Here are some of the steps we're taking towards Pluto's renewed recognition as a planet!

Pluto Segments for Sale

In order to get Pluto back on the registry of planets, we have to increase its net worth! Invest a minimum of $250 in exchange for ownership of 0.01 sq ft of Pluto (totally legit, don't hold out!)

Hacking NASA

Our totally real and existing team of hackers are undyingly commited to having Pluto's back! They spend hours and hours attempting to hack into NASA's official databases in hopes of forcefully reinstating Pluto as a planet!

Jailing Anti-Plutonians

All things considered, jail is too good for these Pluto haters, but alas, it's the worst we can do to them! Our team is actively seeking out anti-plutonians, meticulously crafting elaborate schemes in attempts to frame these pluto haters for any crime we can pin on them! Can I get a "Hell yeah, justice rules!"
